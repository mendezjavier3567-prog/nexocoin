# Nexocoin — Official Branding Guidelines

## Official Logo

The Nexocoin official logo is the circular metallic emblem featuring the stylized "N" symbol.

This logo is final and definitive.

No alternative logos, variations, or redesigns are authorized.

## Logo Usage

The Nexocoin logo must be used as provided, without modification.

### Allowed
- Use on dark or neutral backgrounds
- Proportional scaling
- Digital and print usage

### Not Allowed
- Color alterations
- Distortion or stretching
- Cropping or partial use
- Visual effects (shadows, glows, gradients)
- Rotation or mirroring

## Color Palette

Primary colors are derived directly from the official logo:

- Metallic Silver (primary)
- Deep Blue (secondary)
- Dark Graphite / Black (background)

Exact color values are defined by the logo asset and must not be altered.

## Typography

Typography must be neutral, modern, and legible.

Recommended categories:
- Sans-serif
- Technical / institutional style
- Web-safe and open-source fonts

No decorative or informal typography is permitted.

## Branding Philosophy

Nexocoin branding prioritizes:
- Institutional clarity
- Technical credibility
- Long-term stability
- Minimalism over marketing

The brand must convey trust, sovereignty, and protocol-level seriousness.

## Governance of Branding

Any branding change requires formal project governance approval.

Unauthorized branding usage is not considered official Nexocoin representation.
